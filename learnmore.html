Nepal's Sustainable Tourism

Nepal's sustainable tourism practices prioritize responsible travel that benefits local communities and preserves natural habitats. The country has been at the forefront of sustainable tourism, with community-based tourism and eco-trekking as two of its prominent initiatives. Community-based tourism allows travelers to immerse themselves in the local culture while supporting the local economy, and eco-trekking promotes low-impact hiking and camping in designated areas. Nepal's waste management and eco-friendly transport initiatives contribute to the sustainable development of local communities and the protection of the natural environment.
