Ecotourism is the solution to overtourism

Over-tourism is a growing problem in many popular tourist destinations around the world. The negative impacts of over-tourism include overcrowding, environmental degradation, and strain on local resources and infrastructure. Eco-tourism, on the other hand, is a sustainable alternative to over-tourism that prioritizes responsible travel practices and conservation efforts. By promoting eco-tourism, destinations can mitigate the negative impacts of over-tourism and create a more sustainable and responsible tourism industry. Eco-tourism focuses on promoting sustainable travel practices, including low-impact activities, support for local communities, and conservation efforts.
